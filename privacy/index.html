Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Muse ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Muse mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID, email address (which may be a relay address if you choose to hide your email), and display name (if provided).
  • User Preferences: Your genre, era, origin, and duration selections made during the movie selection ritual.
  • Signature Data: When you sign "The Pact," your signature is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the App, including movie picks, watch history (The Archive), and interaction patterns.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for crash reporting and analytics.
  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics by Google to understand app usage and improve stability.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • RevenueCat: If you subscribe to Muse Premium, RevenueCat processes your subscription. We receive your subscription status but do not have access to your payment details.
  • The Movie Database (TMDB): Movie information displayed in the App (titles, posters, synopses) is sourced from TMDB's API.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • Personalize your movie recommendations based on your selections
  • Sync your archive and picks across your devices via Firebase
  • Process and manage your premium subscription
  • Send push notifications (only if you opt in)
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends to improve the experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and crashes

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using the following services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages your identity securely via Google's infrastructure.
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your user profile, pick history, and deal records in Google's cloud.
  • On-Device Storage (SwiftData): Your archive, signature data, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google), RevenueCat, and TMDB, solely for the purposes of operating the App.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view your data within the App (profile, archive).
  • Deletion: You can delete your account at any time from the Profile page. This permanently removes all your data from Firebase and our servers.
  • Notifications: You can enable or disable push notifications from the Profile page or your device Settings.
  • Sign-In: You may use the App as a guest (anonymous) without providing personal information. Guest data is limited to a device-specific anonymous identifier.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Firebase and Google Cloud services operate globally. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
